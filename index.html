Film Press Kits

Many film festivals require a press kit in pdf format as part of the submission process. This press kit showcases the film's specifications, cast, and credits. Additional elements may include movie stills, film awards and laurels, and a director's statement. A digital press kit serves as a valuable tool for promoting your movie at film festivals. It provides fans with detailed insights into your film and its cast. The press kit includes a QR code that can be scanned with a smartphone for easy access.
